What is GST?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is an indirect tax applied on the supply of goods and services in India. It replaced multiple indirect taxes and simplified the taxation system.

GST Slabs in India

  • 5% GST
  • 12% GST
  • 18% GST
  • 28% GST

Types of GST

  • CGST - Central GST
  • SGST - State GST
  • IGST - Integrated GST

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate GST?

GST is calculated by multiplying the amount with the GST percentage.

What is Reverse GST?

Reverse GST helps find the original amount before GST was added.

What is the difference between CGST, SGST and IGST?

CGST and SGST are used for intra-state transactions while IGST applies to inter-state transactions.
