This free Age Calculator helps you calculate your exact age in years, months and days. Simply select your date of birth and click the Calculate Age button. The calculator instantly shows your current age, total days lived and the remaining days until your next birthday.

What Is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is an online tool that calculates a person's age based on their date of birth. It provides accurate results in years, months and days. Age calculators are useful for school admissions, job applications, official documents and personal use.
