What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measurement that uses your height and weight to estimate whether your body weight falls within a healthy range.

BMI Categories

  • Below 18.5 – Underweight
  • 18.5 to 24.9 – Normal Weight
  • 25 to 29.9 – Overweight
  • 30 and Above – Obese
